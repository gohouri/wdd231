Basilique Notre-Dame de la Paix
One of the largest churches in the world, modeled after St. Peter’s Basilica in the Vatican. Located in Yamoussoukro, it is a major religious and architectural landmark. Visitors can admire its beautiful design.

Félix Houphouët-Boigny International Airport
Situated in Abidjan, this is the main international airport of Côte d'Ivoire. It serves as a hub for both regional and international flights, handling millions of passengers annually.

Ébimpé Olympic Stadium
Also known as Stade Alassane Ouattara, this multi-purpose stadium is located in the Abobo-Ébimpé area of Abidjan. With a capacity of 60,000, it hosts major sports events and concerts.

Motorway North Ivory Coast
The first motorway in Côte d'Ivoire, connecting Abidjan to Bouaké via Yamoussoukro. It enhances trade and travel between major cities and neighboring countries.

Soubré hydroelectric dam
Inaugurated on November 2, 2017, this hydroelectric dam is located near the city of Soubré on the Sassandra River. It has an installed capacity of 275 megawatts, making it the largest hydroelectric facility in the country.

LDS Abidjan Temple
The first temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Côte d'Ivoire, located in Abidjan. It serves as a spiritual center for Latter-day Saints in West Africa.
