The Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Côte d'Ivoire (CCI-CI) is an institution that represents the interests of commercial, industrial, and service companies in the country. Established on December 23, 1908, in Grand-Bassam, it has evolved over the years to adapt to the changing economic landscape. Key Functions of CCI-CI:

Consulting and Representation: Acting as an intermediary between public authorities and the private sector, the CCI-CI ensures that the interests of businesses are effectively represented.

Training: Providing educational programs and resources to entrepreneurs and business professionals to enhance their skills and knowledge.

Assistance and Consulting: Offering support and advisory services to businesses to promote growth and development.

Management of General Interest Services: Creating and managing services that benefit the broader business community. The CCI-CI plays a pivotal role in supporting and advocating for businesses in Côte d'Ivoire, contributing to the country's economic development.
